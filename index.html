Chess Rules

To play chess at Chickadee's, we recommend two modes:

  • In-Game Chat: using /tell or by forming a party with your opponent, take turns writing your moves in standard algebraic notation to each other. Meanwhile, play IRL on a real board or use a digital board with no engine. Our digital board below should give you all you need to do this. We recommend playing friendly games with no time limits in this mode.
  • Online Chess: share your username on chess.com, lichess.org, or equivalent and challenge each other to play on there. This option enables easier short time controls, and can include ranked games if both players want.
